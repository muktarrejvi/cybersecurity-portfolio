### Question 1
Which type of malware is designed to hide its presence by modifying the operating system kernel?

A. Trojan  
B. Worm  
C. Rootkit  
D. Ransomware  

**Correct Answer:** ??  
**Explanation:** Rootkits hide malicious activity and often operate at the kernel level.

---

### Question 2
An attacker sends an email pretending to be the IT department requesting a password reset. What attack is this?

A. Spear phishing  
B. Impersonation  
C. Pretexting  
D. Whaling  

**Correct Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Impersonation involves pretending to be a trusted entity.

---

### Question 3
Which control BEST prevents unauthorized applications from running on an endpoint?

A. Antivirus  
B. Application whitelisting  
C. Host-based firewall  
D. IDS  

**Correct Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** Whitelisting allows only approved applications to execute.

---

### Question 4
Which malware type encrypts files and demands payment to restore access?

A. Virus  
B. Worm  
C. Ransomware  
D. Spyware  

**Correct Answer:** C  

---

### Question 5
What endpoint technology provides continuous monitoring and automated response?

A. HIPS  
B. Antivirus  
C. EDR  
D. TPM  

**Correct Answer:** C  
**Explanation:** EDR detects, investigates, and responds to endpoint threats.

---

### Question 6
Which countermeasure BEST protects against zero-day exploits?

A. Signature-based antivirus  
B. Patch management  
C. Behavior-based detection  
D. Hash verification  

**Correct Answer:** C  
**Explanation:** Zero-days have no signatures; behavior-based detection is effective.

---
